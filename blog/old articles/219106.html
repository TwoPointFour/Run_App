Tips on Running

Before starting any running program, you should make sure your body is ready to take the pounding that will inevitably come with running. You want to have a proper warm up and cool down before and after each workout. This allows for your muscles to be warmed up and properly stretched. A proper running program is important to ensure that you are exercising the right muscles at the right time so that you can avoid injury.
