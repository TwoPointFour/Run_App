Our Technology
TwoPointFour is a fully automated running app providing world's first data driven personalised running programme to optimise results for any runners, regardless of their starting level. TwoPointFour adapts training plans based on your performance to provide the best outcome and help you achieve your goals faster.
TwoPointFour offers the most effective running training in the world: 42% faster in 5K and 42% faster in 10K. TwoPointFour can be your edge to perform better, get fit, stay motivated and have fun.
Our running training programmes are for the long term runner. The programmes are developed in close partnership with elite exercise-scientists with a sport-science foundation, who ensure that every run is challenging and will lead to performance gains for runners of all levels. The workouts are interconnected and can be varied in difficulty level making them challenging to a high or low level runner. In order to master the challenges, we provide two types of coaching that are delivered via our state-of-art technology as well as tailor-made coaching from experienced coaches in your city.
